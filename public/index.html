Rules of Exchange (RoX)

1. I create consciously from the heart, or I leave.

2. If you don't believe design is everything, let's save time and NOT work together.

3. I think big. I push boundaries. I can be a little abstract and very intuitive.

4. I require space and trust to do my best work.

5. I am ambitious and a little secretive. Know I'm always closer to you than you think.

6. I obesses over important details that impact my integrity.

7. $$$ is great. Real profitability for me = financially, spiritually, socially and professionally.

9. Verbal inputs = visual outputs. More questions = less assumptions. Let's talk more.

10. I am serious about my money, my wealth, my creative currency and my value.

11. Every collaboration is an investment. I'm in my 30s and need high returns on errrrything!! :)

12. I need collaborations that make me better, greater and different. Let's grow!
